Our compliance with Google Play Store Developer Policies

← Back to Home

As a Google Play developer, Kid Moments complies with all Google Play Developer Program Policies. Below are our commitments:

1. Intellectual Property

All content (audio, text, graphics) on Kid Moments is original or properly licensed. We respect intellectual property rights and do not infringe on others' copyrights, trademarks, or patents.

2. Privacy & Data Collection

We collect minimal data and only what's necessary. Our full data practices are disclosed in our Privacy Policy. We do not sell user data to third parties.

3. Child Safety (Families Policy)

Kid Moments is designed for children and complies with Google Play's Families Policy requirements:

  • No inappropriate content for children
  • No deceptive or manipulative ads directed at children
  • No collection of personal data from children without parental consent
  • Age-appropriate content in all episodes

4. Monetization Transparency

Any purchases, subscriptions, or in-app purchases are clearly described before purchase. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges. Pricing is displayed in local currency.

5. Ad Policy Compliance

All ads displayed comply with Google Play ad policies:

  • No misleading or disruptive advertising
  • No ads that mimic app functionality
  • Ads are clearly distinguished from app content
  • No interstitial ads that interrupt user experience unexpectedly

6. Permissions Justification

We only request permissions that are essential for app functionality. Each permission is clearly justified in our app listing and privacy policy.

7. Content Rating

Our app is rated "Everyone" through the IARC (International Age Rating Coalition) content rating system. Content is appropriate for all ages.

8. Accurate Store Listing

Our Google Play store listing accurately represents our app's features, screenshots, and description. We do not use misleading metadata or screenshots.

9. No Malware or Deceptive Behavior

Kid Moments contains no malware, spyware, or deceptive code. Our app does not attempt to trick users or misrepresent its functionality.

10. User-Generated Content

If user reviews or comments are enabled, we moderate content to ensure it remains appropriate for a family-friendly audience.

11. Accessibility

We strive to make our app accessible to all users, including those with disabilities, following Google's accessibility guidelines.

12. Updates & Maintenance

We regularly update our app to fix bugs, improve performance, and ensure compatibility with the latest Android versions. Critical security patches are applied promptly.

13. Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion at any time. We comply with Google Play's account deletion requirements and process requests within 30 days.

14. Data Safety Disclosure

Our Data Safety section on Google Play accurately discloses:

  • What data we collect and why
  • Whether data is shared with third parties
  • Security practices in place
  • Data deletion options available to users

15. Compliance & Reporting

We actively monitor our compliance with Google Play policies and promptly address any reported issues. If you believe our app violates any policy, please contact us or report through Google Play.
