Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

← Back to Home

1. Overview

At Kid Moments, we strive to provide the best experience for children and families. Since our podcast is free to listen to, this policy covers any future paid services, in-app purchases, premium content, or merchandise.

🎧 Free Content: Our podcast episodes are completely free. There are no charges for listening, streaming, or browsing our website.

2. Premium Subscriptions (If Applicable)

If we introduce premium or subscription-based content in the future:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • You will retain access to premium content until the end of your paid period
  • No partial refunds for unused portions of a billing cycle

3. Digital Content Refunds

For any paid digital content (e.g., downloadable episodes, bonus content):

  • Refund requests must be made within 14 days of purchase
  • If the content has been downloaded or streamed, a refund may not be possible unless the content is defective
  • Defective content will be replaced or refunded at our discretion

4. In-App Purchases

For purchases made through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store:

  • Refund requests must be directed to the respective store (Google Play or Apple)
  • Each store has its own refund policy and process
  • We will cooperate with the store to resolve any legitimate refund requests
  • Google Play: Visit Google Play Support
  • Apple App Store: Visit reportaproblem.apple.com

5. Merchandise (If Applicable)

If we offer physical merchandise in the future:

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Refund issued within 5–10 business days after receiving the return
  • Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer (unless the item is defective)
  • Defective items will be replaced free of charge including shipping

6. Donations

Any donations made to support Kid Moments are non-refundable. We appreciate your support in keeping our content free for all families.

7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for any eligible purchase:

  1. Visit our Contact page
  2. Select "Refund Request" as the subject
  3. Include your order/transaction ID
  4. Describe the reason for your refund request

We will review your request and respond within 3–5 business days.

8. Contact Us

Questions about our refund policy? Get in touch.
