App Store Terms

Our compliance with Apple App Store Review Guidelines

← Back to Home

As an Apple App Store developer, Kid Moments complies with all Apple App Store Review Guidelines. Below are our commitments:

1. Safety — Objectionable Content

Kid Moments contains zero objectionable content. All episodes are designed for children ages 3–10 and are free from violence, profanity, mature themes, or inappropriate material.

2. User Generated Content

If any user-generated content features are enabled (reviews, comments), we include filtering, reporting mechanisms, and the ability to block abusive users. All content is moderated for child safety.

3. Kids Category Requirements

Our app complies with Apple's Kids Category guidelines:

  • No links out of the app without parental gate
  • No behavioral advertising
  • No in-app purchases without parental consent
  • Age-appropriate content throughout

4. Privacy — Data Collection & Use

We comply with Apple's privacy requirements:

  • Privacy nutrition labels are accurately completed
  • Data collection is limited to what's necessary
  • App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework is implemented
  • Full disclosure in our Privacy Policy

5. In-App Purchases

All digital purchases use Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not offer alternative payment methods within the app. Purchase confirmation and clear pricing are always displayed.

6. Subscriptions

If subscription services are offered:

  • Clear description of what subscribers receive
  • Subscription duration and price clearly displayed
  • Easy cancellation through iOS Settings
  • Free trial terms clearly explained

7. Design — Spam & Clones

Kid Moments is an original application. We do not submit spam, clone, or duplicate apps to the App Store. Our content and design are unique.

8. App Completeness

Our app is fully functional when submitted. It includes all advertised features, working links, complete content, and a polished user interface. No placeholder content or broken functionality.

9. Accurate Metadata

Our App Store listing accurately represents the app:

  • Description matches actual functionality
  • Screenshots are from the real app
  • Keywords are relevant and accurate
  • App category correctly reflects the app's purpose

10. Hardware Compatibility

Kid Moments is designed to work properly on all supported iOS devices and versions listed in our compatibility requirements. We test on multiple screen sizes and device types.

11. Software Requirements

We comply with Apple's software requirements including:

  • Using public APIs only
  • No private API usage
  • Compatible with the latest iOS versions
  • Proper use of Apple frameworks and services

12. Intellectual Property

All content is original or properly licensed. We do not use copyrighted material without permission and do not infringe on third-party trademarks.

13. Content Ratings

Our content rating is set to "4+" as all content is suitable for all ages. We accurately answer all age rating questions in App Store Connect.

14. Account Deletion

Users can delete their account and all associated data directly within the app, as required by Apple's App Store guidelines. Deletion is processed within 30 days.

15. Compliance & Support

We maintain ongoing compliance with Apple's guidelines and promptly address any App Store review feedback. For questions or concerns about our App Store policies:
